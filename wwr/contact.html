White Water Adventures Rafting Company

Our mission is to provide an unforgettable white water rafting experience, ensuring safety, adventure and fun for all clients. We are dedicated in promoting eco-friendly tourism and supporting our local communities as we offer them thrills and bring about a great cause to relieve stress give our clients numberless reasons to come back.

History

Founded in 1982, White Water Adventures Company has grown from a small local business to one of the most respected names in adventure tourism. Our journey began when Jean and Julia Jones decided to turn their passion for rafting into a profession. With a commitment to safety, excitement, and environmental stewardship, we have been offering unforgettable rafting experiences for over 40 years

In the early days, our operations were limited to a few local rivers. However, as word of our exceptional service and thrilling adventures spread, we expanded our offerings to include a variety of rivers and rafting experiences suitable for all skill levels. Our guides, are highly trained professionals dedicated to ensuring that every trip is both safe and exhilarating

Over the years, we have invested in the latest equipments and training to maintain the highest standards of safety and customer satisfaction. Our commitment to the environment has also led us to implement sustainable practices, ensuring that the natural beauty of rivers we raft is preserved for future generations

Today, White Water Adventures Company is proud to be a leader in the adventure tourism industry, offering a range of trips that cater to both beginners and experienced rafters. Join us and become a part of our story!
