Featured Trips

Trip 1: Intermediate

Experience the thrill of navigating through challenging rapids amidst breathtaking scenery. This full-day adventure is perfect for those seeking an adrenaline-packed day on the river. Our expert guides will ensure your safety while you enjoy the rush of the rapids and the beauty of the surrounding wilderness.

Trip 2: Beginner

Ideal for beginners and families, this half-day trip offers a taste of white water rafting without committing to a full day. You'll navigate through milder rapids, enjoying the serenity of the river and the opportunity to spot local wildlife. It's a perfect introduction to the excitement of rafting for all ages

Trip 3: Advanced

Embark on an unforgettable overnight adventure where you'll raft during the day and camp under the stars at night. This trip is designed for experienced rafters looking to challenge themselves with more technical rapids and immerse themselves in the wilderness. Our guides will lead you through exhilarating sections of the river while ensuring your safety and providing insights into the local ecosystem.
